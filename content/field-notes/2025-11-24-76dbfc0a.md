Database connection pool reached maximum capacity during traffic spike. Temporary service degradation occurred.

During marketing campaign, connection pool exhausted causing timeouts. Root cause: Misconfigured max connections based on outdated load assumptions. Mitigation: Doubled connection pool size, implemented connection pooling circuit breaker, added better monitoring and alerting.
